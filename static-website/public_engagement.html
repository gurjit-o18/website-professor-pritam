Community Engagement

  • Advisor to the Oxford Sikh community for over 15 years in the campaign to secure a permanent place of Sikh worship in Oxford.
  • As convenor of the Oxford South Asia Forum, organised discussions on South Asian issues in Oxford.
  • Worked with Asylum Welcome, Campaign to Close Campsfield Detention Centre, and the Medical Foundation for the Victims of Torture.
  • Member of Oxfordshire Standing Advisory Council for Religious Education (2007–present).
  • Member, International Ethics Review and Advisory Panel for Ensaaf's Punjab Documentation Project (2009–13).
  • Chair, Panel of Judges to select eminent Punjabis to be honoured by the House of Commons' All Party Parliamentary Committee (2005–08).
