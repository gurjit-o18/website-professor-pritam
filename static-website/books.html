Books

Federalism, Nationalism and Development: India and the Punjab Economy

Author(s): Pritam Singh
Year: 2008
Publisher: Routledge
ISBN: 978-0415568654

Special Indian Reprint 2009, Second edition 2019. Brings together groundbreaking methodological approaches and field level data to add new dimensions to the understanding of federal modes of governance and the development process, particularly in India. 'The book is one of those rare academic publications which have the potential to make history' (Economic and Political Weekly)

Economy, Culture and Human Rights: Turbulence in Punjab, India and Beyond

Author(s): Pritam Singh
Year: 2010
Publisher: Three Essays Collective
ISBN: 978-8188789221

Marshals diverse theory and evidence drawn from economics, political science, moral philosophy, and history to make a case for understanding human rights as intrinsically important rather than simply as a means to achieve economic and political goals. Subject of extended TV interview on Sky/Sikh Channel
